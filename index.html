About the Camp

Join us for a transformative experience at the Summer Youth Camp, hosted by the Watchman Catholic Charismatic Renewal Movement (WCCRM).

Our camp is designed to provide a safe and nurturing environment for youth to grow in their faith, build friendships, and develop leadership skills.

The camp aims to give children of all ages, from elementary school to college, a chance to get closer to God. Youths and children will receive small-group teaching based on their age range. All those seeking a connection with God are welcome. .

We will have a variety of activities, including worship sessions, workshops, games, and outdoor adventures. Our dedicated team of leaders and volunteers are committed to making this camp a memorable and impactful experience for all participants.

Topic: "Godly youth in a godless world"

We look forward to seeing you at the camp!
